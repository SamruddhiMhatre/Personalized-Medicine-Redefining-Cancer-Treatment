About Classes



The classes represent different levels of whether or not the variant is a driver or a passenger mutation and not the type of cancer. MSKCC has divided the classes into 9 numbers of classes where the initial classes starting from 1 has majority of suppressors (cancer preventing genes) and as we move higher, the number of drivers increases as the class number increases.
